Definitions

See also: Cranial Reference Points and Planes, Human BioMedia, and Anatomy and Biomechanics (CARTA)

Upper facial height: Measured as the linear distance between midline landmarks nasion and prosthion (just above upper central incisor crowns), excluding the lower jaw.

Face width: Distance (mm) between left and right zygomatic (Zy), the most prominent point of the zygomatic bone.

Cranial length (antero-posterior): Distance (mm) between G (glabella) and Op (Opisthocranion, the furthest point from glabella in the sagittal plane).

Cranial width: Distance (mm) between left and right Eurion (Eu), the most lateral points of the neurocranium.

Location of Max Skull Breadth: Divide A (height of cranial width from bottom of cranium) by B (total cranial height).

Supraorbital width: Width of supraorbital arch, from leftmost to rightmost margin.

Postorbital constriction: Width of skull past the supraorbital torus, from leftmost to rightmost margin.

Prognathism: Measured by the craniofacial angle (Frankfort horizontal vs. sellion-prosthion line). Acute in humans, obtuse in great apes.
